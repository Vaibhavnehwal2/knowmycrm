## About Fortech Syngenuity

Fortech Syngenuity is a certified Salesforce partner headquartered in Cluj-Napoca, Romania. Founded in 2021, the company combines strategy, technology, and Salesforce expertise to deliver customized, integrated solutions for businesses across departments and functions.

With a team of certified experts, Fortech Syngenuity serves clients internationally while maintaining a strong presence in Central and Eastern Europe (CEE).

## Core Services

Fortech Syngenuity offers a full spectrum of Salesforce services:

- **Consulting** — Strategic advice on Salesforce adoption and optimization, business process analysis, and roadmap creation for growth and scalability
- **Implementation** — End-to-end Salesforce implementation including system configuration, data migration, user training, and customization aligned to business processes
- **Automation** — Workflow automation using Salesforce Flow and both code and no/low-code features to reduce errors and improve efficiency
- **Integration** — Seamless connections between Salesforce, ERP systems, and department tools using MuleSoft or custom REST/SOAP integrations
- **App Development** — Custom apps built with Lightning App Builder, tailored to specific business needs with scalable, intuitive interfaces
- **Training** — Hands-on training programs for beginners, advanced users, and administrators to maximize Salesforce ROI
- **QA** — Rigorous testing by Salesforce & ISTQB-certified QA team to ensure reliable, bug-free solutions before deployment
- **Managed Services** — Dedicated support with proactive monitoring, updates, and continuous improvements for ongoing optimization

## Delivery Approach

Fortech Syngenuity follows a structured six-phase delivery process:

1. **Kick-Off** — Understand business needs and objectives, gathering key insights for strategic alignment
2. **Define** — Collaboratively design technical requirements and solution architecture
3. **Develop** — Transform requirements into tailored, scalable Salesforce solutions
4. **Test** — Execute rigorous testing and quality checks before deployment
5. **Launch** — Seamlessly deploy the solution with functional features and integrations
6. **Support** — Long-term support ensuring continuous optimization as the business evolves
